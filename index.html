About Artman Contracting

Why Choose Us

We are always there when the time comes to build, repair, remodel, and maintain your home. We are using the best tools and materials of the market to achieve the longest lasting and highest quality work, being as efficient and clean as possible in every aspect of our work.

What We Can Do?

Our Services

Electrical

From switches replacement and light fixtures to any electrical needs of your house.

Plumbing

Need your faucet replaced? Or maybe a new toilet installed? Yes, we do that.

Framing

The first step to finish your basement is to frame the space. Or you maybe need a fence?

Painting

Refresh your rental property before the new tenant or make your home feels like new.

Drywall Mud & Tape

It doesn't matter for us if you finish your basement or you have damaged drywall. We can install or repair the drywall and mud and tape the area to make it ready for paint.

Appliances Installation

Furniture Assembly

Kitchen cabinets, bathroom vanities, Murphy beds. Make your list and we are ready to do it for you.
